Track Record

Organizations we’ve worked with

Short case narratives, not a logo wall. Each engagement required systems-level thinking across engagement, capability, portability, and opportunity.

Workforce Learning

IKEA

Leadership learning programs designed to produce visible behavior change across a global retail organization.

The Challenge

IKEA needed leadership development programs that went beyond knowledge delivery. Leaders at multiple levels needed to demonstrate new behaviors on the job — not just complete modules.

The Approach

We designed learning systems where capability development was tied to real operational challenges. Evidence of behavior change was built into the program architecture.

The Outcome

Leadership programs that produced demonstrable behavior change visible to senior leadership. Programs ran across multiple countries with participant satisfaction above 90%.

Higher Education

Minerva University

Global experiential learning at a university built to prove graduates possess real capabilities.

The Challenge

Minerva’s model required that every student demonstrate capability through real-world application across multiple countries and contexts.

The Approach

We worked on learning systems where experiential components were architecturally integrated with assessment and evidence.

The Outcome

A learning architecture where student capability was evidenced through real performance across seven countries. Graduate employment rates exceeded traditional university benchmarks.

Higher Education

Zayed University UAE

Institutional transformation: redesigning programs to produce career-ready graduates with demonstrable evidence.

The Challenge

Zayed University undertook a major institutional transformation to ensure programs produced graduates whose capabilities could be demonstrated to employers and national stakeholders.

The Approach

We designed a systems-level approach that addressed curriculum architecture, faculty development, assessment redesign, and evidence systems simultaneously.

The Outcome

An institutional learning architecture that aligned governance, faculty practice, and evidence systems around a shared definition of graduate capability.

EdTech

EdTech Advisory

Product-learning fit, impact evidence, and growth strategy across multiple EdTech companies and geographies.

The Pattern

Across advisory engagements with EdTech companies in the US, UK, and Middle East, we consistently find the same structural challenge: products that work but cannot demonstrate learning outcomes.

The Approach

We help EdTech companies build product-learning fit: the evidence connection between product features and demonstrable outcomes.

The Outcome

Stronger renewal conversations, credible impact claims for sales and marketing, and product roadmaps informed by learning science rather than feature requests.

Who we work with: boards, university presidents, provosts, CEOs, CHROs, L&D leaders, EdTech founders, and product leaders.
